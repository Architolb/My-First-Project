Who AM I?

I am glad you asked!

My name is Brandon Tolbert and I am a software engineer. I create value with my code and what it can do for you. I am a Texas based Army veteran who has been excelling in the tech industry for the past 7 years. I graduated from Rice University with my B.S in Biomechanics and Kinesiology. I worked for a food brokerage in Houston, Tx for a year as an administrtive consultant. After a year of that I decided to use my degree and started work as a Physical therapy technician in Dallas, Tx helping recently injured clients get back to a better quality of life. I also became a personal trainer at Gold's Gym training clients reach their optimum potetntial in weight loss and muscle gain. I excelled as a personal trainer and was awarded the distinction of being the best Golds Personal trainer in the entire Southwest Region of the US. I soon set my sites on serving my country and decided to join the Army. I joined the armed forces as a multichannel communications operator and maintainer. Alot of words to say I worked with Satellites to ensure our troops on the ground had reliable communications to our allies. I spent a little over 4 years in the Army and achieved all I wanted to achieve and decided it was time to go back to the civilian sector in order to be home more for my wife and I's newly born son. I took the knowledge I gained from the military and began my carrer in IT Support where I cut my teeth in the tech arena. I fell in love and never looked back. earned a postion with Amazon AWS as a Premium Support Engineer. I am currently learning as much as I can about different coding languages (Python and Java Script) in order to improve my coding skill set. I look forward to the hardwork and and dedication it will take to turn me into the future coder I aim to be!

My Hobbies!

Here is what I like to do outside of coding!

I love to learn new things primarily new technology. My family considers me the trivia guy as I know a little about mostly everything or can quickly rejog my memory on subjects i have previously researched. I like solving puzzles, playing brain teaser games, and learning about different cultures. I am an avid traveler who can't help but plan our next vacation before we even get back from our current one! I hope to one day ttravel to at least 5 different continents. With traveling comes my other hobby of staying fit. From running on the beadch in Tulum to lifting weights at muscle beach in California I can find anyplace to get in a good sweat. Not only is it good for my body, but I feel more alert and in better spirits everytime I work out. The ability to alleviate stress and forget about the struggles and blockers of the day is vital for me. Another reason why I like to work out is because I like to cook and eat!
